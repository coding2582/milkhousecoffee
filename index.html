Our Story

More Than Coffee.
It's Community.

"We built The Milk House to be a warm, welcoming spot where people can slow down and enjoy something good."

When we found this hidden building off Rucker, we knew it had something special. Even with all the uncertainty during the pandemic, we took the risk and made it ours in 2020. As we worked on it, we discovered its history as Walt's Milk House and tried to keep that same community feeling alive. Now it's more than just a coffee shop. It's a place where people feel at home and become part of the story.

Handcrafted with Care

Every drink made to order, every morning, with quality beans and real skill.

Rooted in Everett

Locally owned since 2020. Proud to serve our neighbors, day after day.

Fresh Every Day

Pastries baked fresh each morning, seasonal flavors that keep things interesting.
